    • Nkanyezi yolwandle Ancestral Boutique(since August 2025) is a unique fashion business that blends modern style with deep cultural roots of ubungoma(divine ancestral healing) and allows spiritually gifted people express their gifts and who they are through fashion. "Nkanyezi yolwandle" means star of the sea. It was named after my great great grandmother who was Miriam, Miriam means 'star of the sea' . She was the one who named me 'Njabulo'. Our Boutique celebrates ubungoma by offering ancestral inspired clothing and customized accessories .



    Mission:

    Our mission as Nkanyezi yolwandle Ancestral Boutique is to honor and preserve ancestral heritage by offering authentic and meaningful pieces that connect our divine healers with their ancestors . We are dedicated to celebrating spirituality and identity through collections that inspire pride ,healing and a deeper connection to one's roots.



    Vision:

    Our vision is to create a sacred boutique that connects people with their roots and spiritual heritage . We aim to preserve ancestral wisdom while blending it with modern lifestyle. We are offering products(clothes) that heals the spirit and celebrate spiritual identity , also that spiritually aligns with the guidance of their ancestors .
